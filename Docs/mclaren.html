About the McLaren 720S

The McLaren 720S is part of the McLaren Super Series, representing the pinnacle of McLaren's performance engineering. With its striking design, groundbreaking technology, and breathtaking speed, the 720S sets a new standard for supercars.

With a 4.0L V8 engine that produces 710 horsepower, the 720S can go from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.8 seconds and has a top speed of 212 mph. The car's lightweight carbon fiber chassis ensures exceptional handling and agility, making it a joy to drive both on the road and on the track.
